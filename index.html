F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can process automation benefit me?

    Process automation will free up your accountant’s time, so they can put more energy into the value-adding activities, and for business owners this will include but not be limited to increased visibility, control, compliance, agility and speed.

    Auto Accounts will help you take one step closer to become a more dynamic business.

  • Can any process be automated?

    The best processes for automation tend to be high-volume, repetitive activities that are easy to understand and organise but largely prone to human error and processes where quality have been inconsistent.

    If you are unsure whether processes are suitable for automation, we will be happy to advise during the consultation period.

  • I’ve heard automation is expensive and only worth it for big companies?

    Generally for SME’s this is not the case as there is minimal scaling issues. Auto Accounts will work with you to design cost effective automation solutions to fit your needs.

    Through applying our automation solutions you will likely see a 40% - 75% reduction in costs with small payback periods.

  • Are there any system requirements?

    We can adapt automation solutions to suit Mac OS or Windows but this will subject to any other business applications you would like in the process.
